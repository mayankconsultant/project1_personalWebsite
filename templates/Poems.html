My Mom's Birthday

Wishing you a "Happy Birthday",
Is a drop of Ocean of my wishes,
For my feelings, as Today,
Dictionary has words in pieces,
So thoughts, dreams and kisses,
let us together cherish,
let me be on your feet,
Before the evening perish.

Shall I be called, "A Momma's Boy",
For days, months and even years to come,
Deeper down the memory lane,
I Know the days of your struggle,
of your Love that shaped me up,
And Your anger, that made me flaw less,
Also your hidden grief, that gave me blessings,
And your hopes for me,
sketched a map, for me to go ahead...

A day shall proclaim,
You to be Proud of me,
As Proud I always am,
to be your son indeed,
But I want that day, you to have a tear,
a tear of happiness flowing down your cheeks...
